Start with Small Steps

How can you make your financial goals smaller so they're less overwhelming?

We all have lofty financial dreams that can seem unrealistic, but often if we break our bigger goals into shorter steps we can build momentum to reach them. I'm also on this journey to achieve my dreams and I'll be sharing my ups and downs along the way.

Mike Tyson once said "Everyone has a plan until they get punched in the mouth" and sometimes personal finance and reaching your goals can feel like that. All the time there's a new unforseen expense, or life throws you a curveball, and it can seem like you'll never achieve those dreams. Below are resources to help you punch back and be financially successful
